Who it's for

Built for every level of
the Ghanaian school system

From nursery to SHS — Ilimi understands the structure, naming conventions and requirements of each school type.

01
Basic Schools
KG, Primary and JHS with Ghana-standard class names like "Primary 3 Nkrumah" and JHS streams.
02
Senior High Schools
SHS elective streams — Science, Business, Arts, Technical and Home Economics — all supported natively.
03
International Schools
Multi-branch support, custom class naming and flexible fee structures for private and international schools.
04
School Groups
Multi-tenant architecture — manage multiple schools and branches under one organisation account.
